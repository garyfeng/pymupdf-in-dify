## Privacy Policy

This plugin processes PDFs only for the duration of each request. Uploaded files and generated outputs remain within the Dify execution environment and are not transmitted to external services.

- No user information is collected or stored outside the execution context.
- PDF content and derived artifacts are removed after the task completes.
- Credentials are not required; no authentication data is retained.
- Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to process uploaded documents.
